## Strength
Measures bodily power, athletic training and the extent to which you can exert raw physical force.

**Athletics**:
- Climb sheer or slippery surface
- Cling to surface when someone is trying to knock you off
- Try to jump long distance or pull off a stunt midjump
- Struggle to swim in treacherous waters

**Other Strength Checks**:
- Force open a door
- Break free of bonds
- Push through a tunnel that is too small
- Hang on to a wagon while being dragged behind it
- Tip over a statue
- Keep a boulder from rolling

**Lifting and Carrying**:
- Carrying Capacity = Strength * 15 lbs.
- Push, Drag or Lift = Carrying Capacity * 2 lbs.
- For each size category above medium, double capacity
- Tiny creatures halve Carrying Capacity

## Dexterity
Measures agility, reflexes and balance.

**Acrobatics**:
- Balance
- Acrobatic stunts, such as dives, rolls somersaults and flips

**Sleight of Hand**:
- Magic tricks
- Conceiling an object on person
- Pickpocket

**Stealth**:
- Conceal oneself
- Sneak past guards
- Slip away
- Sneak up on someone

**Other Dexterity Checks**:
- Control a heavily laden cart on a steep descent
- Steer a chariot around a tight turn
- Pick a lock
- Disable a trap
- Securely tie up a prisoner
- Wriggle free of bonds
- Play a stringed instrument
- Craft a small or detailed object
- Combat Initiative

## Constitution
Measures health stamina and vital force.

**Constitution Checks**
- Hold breath
- March or labor for hours without rest
- Go without sleep
- Survive without food or water
- Quaff an entire stein of ale in one go

## Intelligence
Measures mental acuity, accuracy of recall, and the ability to reason.

**Arcane**:
- Recall lore about spells
- Recall lore about magic items
- Recall lore about eldritch symbols
- Recall lore about magical traditions
- Recall lore about planes of existence
- Recall lore about inhabitants of those planes

**History**:
- Recall lore about historical events
- Recall lore about legendary people
- Recall lore about ancient kingdoms
- Recall lore about past disputes
- Recall lore about recent wars
- Recall lore about lost civilizations

**Investigation**:
- Deduce location of a hidden object
- Discern from appearance of wound what kind of weapon dealt it
- Determine weakest point in tunnel that can cause it to collapse

**Nature**:
- Recall lore about terrain
- Recall lore about plants and animals
- Recall lore about weather
- Recall lore about natural cycles

**Religion**:
- Recall lore about deities
- Recall lore about rites and prayers
- Recall lore about religious hierarchies
- Recall lore about holy symbols
- Recall lore about practices of secret cults

**Other Intelligence Checks**:
- Communicate with a creature without using words
- Estimate the value of a precious item
- Pull together a disguise to pass as a city guard
- Forge a document
- Recall lore about a craft or trade
- Win a game of skill

## Wisdom
Reflects how attuned you are to the world around you and represents perceptiveness and intuition

**Animal Handling**:
- Calm down a domesticated animal
- Keep a mount from getting spooked
- Intuit an animal's intentions
- Control mount to attempt risky maneuver

**Insight**:
- Determine true intentions of a creature
- Search out a lie
- Predict someone's next move

**Medicine**:
- Stabilize a dying companion
- Diagnose an illness

**Perception**:
- Detect the presence of something
- Eavesdrop
- Spot something that is obscured
- Spot an ambush

**Survival**: 
- Follow tracks
- Hunt wild game
- Guide group
- Identify animal signs
- Predict weather
- Avoid quicksand and other natural hazards

**Other Wisdom Checks**:
- Get a gut feeling about what course of action to follow
- Discern whether a seemingly dead or living creature is undead

## Charisma
Measures your ability to interact effectively with others

**Deception**:
- Hide the truth
- Fast talk a guard
- Con a merchant
- Earn money through gambling
- Pass yourself off in a disguise
- Dull someone's suspicions
- Maintain a straight face while telling a blatant lie

**Intimidation**:
- Influence someone through over threats, hostile actions or physical violence
- Pry information out of a prisoner
- Convincing streat thugs to back down from a confrontation

**Performance**:
- Delight an audience through some form of entertainment

**Persuasion**:
- Influence someone with tact, social graces or good nature
- Foster friendship
- Make cordial request
- Exhibit proper etiquette

**Other Charisma Checks**:
- Find best person to talk to for news, rumors and gossip
- Blend into a crowd to get the sense of key topics of conversation
